Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-27

Summary

EscobaDel15 does not collect, store, or share personal data.

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Data Collection

EscobaDel15 does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, location, identifiers, or usage analytics.

Game settings and progress, if any, are stored locally on the user’s device.

Gameplay Data

EscobaDel15 is a mobile card game based on Escoba del 15. The app may store gameplay-related data locally on the device to support features such as saved settings, match progress, or preferences.

This data is not sold or shared with third parties.

Purchases

If in-app purchases or subscriptions are offered, they are handled by Apple through In-App Purchases. EscobaDel15 does not receive or store payment details.
